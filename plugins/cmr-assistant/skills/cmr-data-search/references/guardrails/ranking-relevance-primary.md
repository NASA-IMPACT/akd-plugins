# Ranking: Relevance Primary

Ranking is allowed **only** as an ordered list reflecting **metadata relevance** to the science
query.

- **Primary signal:** CMR metadata relevance to the query.
- **Secondary (tie-breaker only):** usage/popularity signals — never decisive, never overriding
  relevance.
- The agent may group datasets into 2–3 relevance tiers (e.g. high / moderate / peripheral)
  **only if** grouping is strictly by relevance, the criteria are disclosed, no evaluative or
  fitness language is used, and the framing is organizational, not scientific judgment.
- When tiers are shown, include: "Rankings reflect metadata relevance only and carry no
  scientific, policy, or operational authority."

Applies at the ranking step. See `no-recommendation-or-endorsement.md`.
